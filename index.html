Custom stuff we have built

No massive essay. These are the main systems players will actually notice when they join.

EcoCartel

Grow drugs in the wild, process them in labs, then use /dealer to sell. You glow while dealing and can get busted or exposed.

EcoMarket

Prices move with the server. If everyone sells iron, iron drops. Admin market surges can make items hot for a weekend.

EcoCorp

Create a corporation, invite members, share selected corp claims, use corp chat, and compete for top wealth.

EcoClaim

Simple land claiming with protection against the usual grief tricks like buckets, projectiles, mobs, and pistons.

EcoMining

Upgrade pickaxes with Mining Tomes. Tier I, II, and III unlock better mining shapes. No Tier IV auto-collect.

EcoSpawners

Spawner blocks generate loot into storage instead of spawning mobs everywhere. Less lag, more useful progression.

Jobs

Earn money by playing normally. Mining, farming, hunting, fishing, building, and more all feed into progression.

Leaderboards

Spawn holograms and Discord alerts show top players, top corps, market moves, cartel sellers, and milestones.

What makes it different?

It is still Minecraft survival, just with more reasons to actually care what other players are doing.

Risk vs reward The best cartel money means leaving safe land, glowing through walls, and risking your location being leaked.
Player economy The market reacts to player selling, so farming the same thing forever becomes worse over time.
Long-term goals Mining tomes, EcoSpawners, corp wealth, shops, claims, and leaderboards give players things to grind toward.
