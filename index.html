Core Server Mechanics

Living Economy

Shop prices rise and fall based on real player supply and demand. Flood the market and watch the price tank. Track trends via a live spawn ticker.

Corporations

Found a company with friends. Share access to corp-owned land automatically, flash your corp tag in Tab/Chat, and dominate the live wealth leaderboard.

Custom Land Claiming

Built from scratch to block griefing vectors (buckets, arrows, pistons, endermen). Start with 256 free blocks and purchase more. PvP remains active outside claims.

Player Shops

Rent premium market stalls at spawn or build storefronts inside your claim. The admin shop sets the price floor—everything above is player-driven trade.

The Cartel

Grow and process 6 illegal substances to sell to street dealers at a 1.5x premium. Beware: dealing builds "heat" from undercover NPC cops. Get caught, lose it all.

EcoMining Upgrades

4 sequential Mining Tome tiers fused at anvils with XP. Progress from a Tier I (3x1 vertical trench) to a Tier IV (3x3x3 cube with automated inventory collection).

EcoSpawners

6 lag-free spawner types generating passive loot directly into storage. Upgrade speed/storage via the Progression Shop. Wither Skeleton spawners are strictly limited to one per player.

13 Passive Jobs

Earn steady income doing what you love. Choose from 13 fully integrated professions including Miner, Farmer, Hunter, Fisherman, Builder, and Enchanter.
