Custom Engineered Plugins

Proprietary mechanics designed for ultimate competitive performance.

EcoCartel High Risk

Grow contraband crops in the wilderness, process them in lab stations, and trigger /dealer mode to sell to street buyer NPCs at a 1.5× premium.

Undercover cops & coordinates leaks active

EcoMarket Dynamic

A live, fluctuating market environment. Item prices rapidly drop when oversold by the community and gradually recover over periods of absolute scarcity. Run /market to track historical trends.

EcoCorp Guilds

Found or join corporate empires via /corp. Establish uniform corporation tags, access shared company land claims seamlessly, communicate via private group chat (/gc), and ascend the real-time corporate wealth leaderboards.

EcoClaim Custom

Built cleanly from scratch to prevent exploitative griefing vectors. Pistons, buckets, projectile targets, and Endermen interactions are explicitly blocked. Access structural controls using /claims.

EcoMining Tomes

Linear, pickaxe-linked anvil upgrade system. Progress sequentially from Tier I (3x1 vertical trenches) to Tier IV (3x3x3 cubic zone excavations paired with instant, lag-free inventory auto-collection layout).

EcoSpawners Lag-Free

Static loot generation systems housed inside container interfaces. Eliminates entity-pathfinding lag entirely. Allows dual upgrades across speed and storage size paths simultaneously. Wither Skeleton variants are strictly capped.

13 Passive Jobs

Earn persistent currency allocations natively by executing core gameplay task trees across Miner, Farmer, Hunter, Fisherman, Builder, and more.

Wilderness PvP

The world outside claims is lethal by design. Elite money-making systems and production labs require risking safety to acquire optimal margins.

Real-time Holograms

Track competitive stats directly at spawn via placeholder-powered hologram leaderboards tracking Baltop, Kills, Playtime, and specialized Cartel earnings.

Content Deployment Timeline

A structured release timeline meticulously organized to preserve progression stability.

Phase 01

Official Server Launch

Initial structural release deployment. Features active integration across EcoCartel production loops, EcoMarket operations, EcoCorp networks, EcoClaims base maps, 13 baseline economy jobs, and complete access to progression paths across EcoSpawners and EcoMining.

Week 2

The Syndicate Update

Introduction of physical "Cash Bundles". Illicit transactions yield unwashed, vulnerable assets. Players are forced to manually transport and hold assets inside public Laundromat zones to fully verify resources while global high-threat proximity announcements reveal transaction coordinates directly to opposing bounty hunters.

Week 3

Entertainment & Draconic Bounties

Integration of the server Casino engine, presenting custom slot machines and high-risk scratch mechanics. Concurrently introduces daily Ender Dragon instances hosting persistent server-wide kill bounties updating directly on the spawn community map boards.

Week 4

Corporate Season Finales

Conclusion of Corporate Season 1 operations. Capital valuations lock automatically across all active networks. Dominant organizations receive premium resource prize tier allocations alongside permanent structural trophies rendered inside spawn's commercial district. Season 2 launches immediately post-payout.
