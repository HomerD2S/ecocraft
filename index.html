EcoCartel High Risk

Grow contraband crops in the wilderness, process them in lab stations, and trigger /drugs to sell to street buyer NPCs at a 1.5× premium.

Undercover cops & coordinates leaks active

EcoMarket Dynamic

A live, fluctuating market environment. Item prices rapidly drop when oversold by the community and gradually recover over periods of absolute scarcity. Run /market to track historical trends.

EcoCorp Guilds

Found corporate empires via /corp. Establish unique corporation tags, access shared company land claims seamlessly, communicate via private group chat (/gc), and ascend the real-time wealth leaderboards.

EcoClaim Custom

Built cleanly from scratch to prevent exploitative griefing vectors. Pistons, buckets, projectile targets, and Endermen interactions are explicitly blocked. Access structural controls using /claims.

EcoMining Tomes

Linear, pickaxe-linked anvil upgrade system. Progress sequentially from Tier I (3x1 vertical trenches) to Tier IV (3x3x3 cubic zone excavations paired with instant, lag-free inventory auto-collection layout).

EcoSpawners Lag-Free

Static loot generation systems housed inside container interfaces. Eliminates entity lag entirely. Allows dual upgrades across speed and storage size paths simultaneously. Wither Skeleton variants are capped.

13 Passive Jobs

Earn persistent currency allocations natively by executing core gameplay tasks across Miner, Farmer, Hunter, Fisherman, and Builder trees.

Real-time Holograms

Track competitive stats directly at spawn via placeholder-powered hologram leaderboards tracking Baltop, Kills, Playtime, and Corporate value assets.

Content Deployment Timeline

A structured release timeline meticulously organized to preserve progression stability.

Phase 01

Official Server Launch

Initial structural release deployment. Features active integration across EcoCartel production loops, EcoMarket operations, EcoCorp networks, EcoClaims base maps, 13 baseline economy jobs, and complete access to progression paths across EcoSpawners and EcoMining.

Week 2

The Syndicate Update

Introduction of physical "Cash Bundles". Illicit transactions yield unwashed, vulnerable assets. Players are forced to manually transport assets inside public Laundromat zones to fully verify resources while global proximity announcements reveal coordinates to bounty hunters.

Week 3

Entertainment & Draconic Bounties

Integration of the server Casino engine, presenting custom slot machines and high-risk scratch mechanics. Concurrently introduces daily Ender Dragon instances hosting chess-matched server kill bounties updating directly on the spawn community map boards.

Week 4

Corporate Season Finales

Conclusion of Corporate Season 1 operations. Capital valuations lock automatically. Dominant organizations receive premium resource prize tier allocations alongside permanent structural trophies rendered inside spawn's commercial district. Season 2 launches instantly.
